about WAJOdesign


Some info about us

WAJO is a term used for elder people in Halabisa Language (one of the 80+ languages in Ethiopia)

WAJOdesign, born in October 2021, is the realization of a passionate dream rooted in a deep love for art and a vision to contribute to the cultural vibrancy of Ethiopia. As the founder, I am a dedicated dreamer, keen on building bridges between the present and the inspiring future I envision.

My commitment extends to creating a dynamic platform that serves as a canvas for showcasing the immense talent and creativity of our remarkable nation. WAJOdesign is not just about my journey but a collective effort to provide a space where individuals, like you, can savor the unique flavors of art they aspire to explore.
